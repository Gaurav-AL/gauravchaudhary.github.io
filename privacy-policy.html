Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  App: Quix  ·  Developer: Quix App

This Privacy Policy explains how Quix ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. By using Quix, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Quix does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details. We collect only what is necessary to run the service:

  • Anonymous Device ID: A random ID assigned by Firebase Anonymous Authentication. Not linked to your name, email, or phone number.
  • Nickname: The name you choose for multiplayer sessions. Temporary and not stored permanently.
  • Usage counters: Number of free and Pro AI quiz generations used, stored under your anonymous ID.
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription and its expiry date.
  • Device information: Basic device data collected by Google AdMob for ad serving.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash logs via Firebase to help us fix bugs.

2. AI Quiz Generation

When you upload a document (PDF, TXT, DOC, DOCX), the text content is sent to the Anthropic Claude API to generate quiz questions. We do not store your document content on our servers. Generated quiz packs are saved locally on your device only.

Important: Do not upload documents containing passwords, financial details, medical records, or any sensitive personal information. Only upload study material and educational content.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

Quix offers a free tier (2 lifetime AI generations), pay-per-generation (₹19 per generation), and a Pro subscription (₹99/month including 10 generations). All payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card number, UPI ID, or any payment credentials.

We store only your subscription status and expiry date in Firebase under your anonymous ID. Payment transaction IDs are stored for verification purposes only.

4. Multiplayer

When you create or join a multiplayer room, the following data is temporarily stored in Firebase Realtime Database:

  • Room code
  • Nickname chosen for the session
  • Your answers and score during the game
  • Online/offline status during the session

All multiplayer room data is automatically deleted from Firebase within 2–6 hours after the session ends.

5. Third-Party Services

Quix uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Anonymous authentication, Realtime Database, Remote Config
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI quiz generation from uploaded documents
  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Google AdMob — In-app advertising
  • Google Play Services — App distribution

6. Data Storage and Security

Quiz packs are stored locally on your device. Subscription and usage data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud) under your anonymous ID, protected by Firebase security rules.

Retention: Multiplayer data deleted within 6 hours. Payment records retained for 90 days. Anonymous user records retained as long as your device ID is active.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. To request deletion of your Firebase records, contact us at the email below.

To opt out of personalised ads: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

8. Children's Privacy

Quix is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The anonymous ID assigned by Firebase contains no personal data. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
