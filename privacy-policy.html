Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · App: Quix

This Privacy Policy explains how Quix ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application. By using Quix, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Quix is designed to work without requiring personal account registration. We collect only limited information necessary for the operation of the app.

  • Anonymous Identifier: A randomly generated identifier assigned to your device that allows the app to function properly. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or phone number.
  • Nickname: A display name you choose for multiplayer sessions. This is used only during gameplay.
  • Usage Data: Limited usage information such as feature usage counters or subscription status required to provide the service.
  • Device Information: Basic device-related data such as device type, operating system version, and app performance information.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical logs that help us identify and fix software errors.

2. AI Quiz Generation

The app allows users to upload documents (such as PDF, TXT, DOC, or DOCX files) in order to generate quiz questions.

When this feature is used, the text content of the document may be processed by secure AI services to generate quiz material. The original document content is not permanently stored on our servers.

Generated quiz packs are typically saved locally on your device.

Important: Please avoid uploading documents containing sensitive personal, financial, medical, or confidential information.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

Quix may offer optional paid features or subscription services. Payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers.

We do not collect or store your card number, UPI details, banking information, or other payment credentials.

Limited information required to verify purchases, such as subscription status or transaction references, may be stored to enable the service.

4. Multiplayer Features

When participating in multiplayer sessions, certain temporary information may be processed to enable gameplay functionality.

  • Room or session codes
  • Player nicknames
  • Answers and scores during gameplay
  • Session activity status

This information is temporary and is automatically removed after the session ends or within a reasonable operational period.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with trusted third-party service providers to support functionality such as authentication, cloud storage, AI processing, analytics, advertising, and payment processing.

These services may process certain information as required to perform their functions and operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Storage and Security

Some information necessary for the operation of the app may be stored on secure cloud infrastructure or locally on your device.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or to comply with applicable legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of certain information associated with your use of the app where applicable.

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

If you wish to request deletion of server-side data or have questions regarding your information, you may contact us using the email provided below.

Users may also control certain advertising preferences through their device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Quix is designed for educational and entertainment purposes and may be used by a wide range of users. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to remove such information.
