Privacy Policy

Quix — Daily Facts Quiz App
Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Quix ("we", "our", or "us") is a free quiz application developed for educational and entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our third-party service providers may collect certain data.

  • Google AdMob: may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Local Storage: Quiz scores and game statistics are stored locally on your device using Shared Preferences. This information never leaves your device.

3. How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To store your quiz scores and game statistics locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertising services. Google may collect and use data according to their privacy policy.

You can read Google's Privacy Policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

Our app includes a Kids Zone designed for children aged 3–7. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Kids Zone section contains no external links and does not collect any personal data.

6. Data Security

All quiz progress data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Payments within the app are processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store any credit card, debit card, or UPI details.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our app store listing.
