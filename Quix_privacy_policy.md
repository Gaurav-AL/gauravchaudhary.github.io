# Privacy Policy for Quix — Daily Facts Quiz App

Last updated: March 8, 2026

### 1. Introduction
Quix ("we", "our", or "us") is a free quiz application developed for educational and entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

#### 3. Information We Collect
We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our third-party service providers may collect certain data:

Google AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant advertisements.
Shared Preferences stores your quiz scores and game statistics locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

### 3. How We Use Information

To display advertisements via Google AdMob
To save your high score and games played count locally on your device

### 4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertising. Google may use your data as described in their Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5. Children's Privacy
   
Our app includes a Kids Zone section designed for children aged 3–7. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Kids Zone contains no external links and no direct data collection.

### 7. Data Security

All quiz progress data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
We collect payment information processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store card or UPI details.

### 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our Play Store listing.
